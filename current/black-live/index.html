archive → current 16–22 → Black Live

    Parts of these compositions were performed at the “Concert in a dark hall”, The Pushkin Museum of Fine Arts, Moscow. An event took place in complete darkness, as music was evolving into an exploration of sonic memory guided by a leading voice. It then transitioned into a screening of a nostalgic video narrative: a cinematographic journey, recorded by fishermen using a palmcorder, as they embarked on prolonged expeditions to the northern seas.
